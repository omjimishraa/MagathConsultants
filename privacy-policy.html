Magath Consultants recognizes the importance of protecting personal data and is committed to maintaining your privacy. This Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Collection and Use of Personal Data

We collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details only as necessary for business purposes. This may include:

  • Delivering services and responding to inquiries
  • Improving our offerings and sharing updates or promotions
  • Conducting research and processing job applications
  • Complying with applicable legal requirements

We may also collect website usage data, including IP address and browsing activity, to analyze performance and enhance user experience. Cookies or similar technologies may be used for this purpose; however, they do not identify individual users.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Access to such data is limited and managed responsibly. Our technical and organizational safeguards are regularly reviewed to align with industry standards and best practices for data security.

Sharing of Personal Data

Personal data may be shared with trusted service providers, business partners, or regulatory authorities only when necessary for business operations or legal compliance. All such parties are expected to adhere to applicable data protection standards. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal data, or withdraw your consent for its use, subject to applicable laws. To exercise these rights, you may contact us directly. We are committed to acknowledging and responding to such requests within a reasonable timeframe.
